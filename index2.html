Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't CBD brands use Stripe or PayPal for subscriptions?

Stripe and PayPal categorize CBD and supplements as high-risk due to federal and banking regulations, often resulting in frozen funds or sudden account termination even for compliant brands.

Which payment processors actually support CBD subscriptions and pass compliance?

Specialized high-risk processors like PaymentCloud, Easy Pay Direct, and NMI are built to handle CBD regulations and recurring billing — with proactive compliance support during onboarding.

What platforms can these processors integrate with?

Many of these processors support Shopify, WooCommerce, Chargebee, and custom stacks via API — allowing seamless billing and checkout flows tailored for high-risk CBD businesses.
